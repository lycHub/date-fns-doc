differenceInCalendarYears

获取给定日期之间的日历年数。 类型 说明 Number 日历年数 
           // How many calendar years are between 31 December 2013 and 11 February 2015?
            var result = differenceInCalendarYears(
              new Date(2015, 1, 11),
              new Date(2013, 11, 31)
            )
            //=> 2
